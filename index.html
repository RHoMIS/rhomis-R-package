1 About

The Rural Household Multi-Indicator Survey (RHoMIS) is designed to collect data on smallholder farmers. The survey is rich in information, covering a range of topics from dietary diversity to livelihoods. With so much information available, it can often be difficult to extract,

This is a book documenting how to use the RHoMIS R-package. We cover 3 main topics:

  1. How to process data collected RHoMIS
  2. The logic of the calculations, and an overview of key indicators/outputs
  3. How to use the R-package to extract useful information
